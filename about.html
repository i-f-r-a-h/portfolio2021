About Ifrah

I am a front-end web developer and designer who is based in Central London, UK. I am currently a student at SAE Institute and online code camps such as Udemy and Freecodecamp where i am developing my skills to design and build digital interfaces and products.

I enjoy sharing my knowledge and helping others adopt the technologies I’m passionate about. I do this by contributing and developing open-source projects as well as writing technical blog posts which can be found within this portfolio and Dev.to.
